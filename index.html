J S Hindu Inter College, Amroha

Previous Next

J S Hindu Inter College has been a leading school in Amroha district, which has been providing excellent education to the students and students of the area under the leadership of the well-qualified teachers and learned Principals of the school.

The leading social worker, the most revered Late Jhabba Lal ji had established the school in 1912. His successor, the most revered Late Jagdish Saran Ji, did a virtuous task of opening the doors of education to the people by getting recognizance the high school in 1936 and the Intermediate Arts, Commerce and Science in 1949.

"Education is the best friend. An educated person is respected everywhere. Education beats the beauty and the youth."

Chanakya

About Our College

अग्रणी समाज सेवी परम श्रद्धेय स्वर्गीय झब्बा लाल जी ने 1912 में विद्यालय की स्थापना की थी | उनके उत्तराधिकारी परम श्रद्धेय स्वर्गीय जगदीश सरन जी ने 1936 में हाईस्कूल तथा 1949 में इंटरमीडिएट की कला , कॉमर्स तथा विज्ञान की मान्यता दिलाकर श्रेत्रवासियो के लिए शिक्षा के द्वार खोलने का पुनीत कार्य किया |
